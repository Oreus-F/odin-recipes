Coconut Chicken Curry

Description

This Creamy Coconut Chicken Curry is delicious, healthy, made in one pot and best of all ready in only 30 minutes! Forget take-out, this is about to become your new family favorite.

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven. Add the chicken to the Dutch oven and season with salt and pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the chicken is no longer pink.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, curry powder and stir. Cook for 2 more minutes then add the chicken broth, coconut milk, tomatoes, tomato paste and sugar. Stir everything together, bring to a boil then cover with a lid. Turn the heat down and simmer for another 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Garnish with parsley and serve over rice.

Prep Time :

5 mins

Cook Time :

25 mins

Total Time :

30 mins

Servings :

6

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 pounds chicken breasts (skinless and boneless, cut into bite size pieces)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper (or to taste)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 14 ounce coconut milk (1 can)
  • 14.5 ounce diced tomatoes (1can)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, chopped

Tips ?

A standard serving size for rice ranges between 1/2 cup and 1 cup per person. The precise amount may vary depending on the rice type being cooked, but generally, 1/2 cup to 1 cup of cooked rice per serving is appropriate.
