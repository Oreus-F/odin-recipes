Toast Avocado and Egg

Description

If you never try an avocado toast, do it. It's really tasty and if you add the egg to it you have a full meal. You can even add some flavor to the avocado with lemon juice.

Prep Time :

5 mins

Cook Time :

5 mins

Total Time :

10 mins

Servings :

2

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices multigrain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, pitted, peeled
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice, or to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • sea salt and ground black pepper to taste
